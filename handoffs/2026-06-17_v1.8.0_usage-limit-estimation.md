### How the "3h" formula works

Rolling 3h windows (like OpenAI's) are more frequent than 5h session blocks. Active-hours formula:
```
windows/month = workingDaysPerMonth × hoursPerSession / 3
              = 20 × 5 / 3 = 33.3
messages/month = 160 × 33.3 = 5,333
tokens/month ≈ 5,333 × 1,125 (base) ≈ 6M
```
